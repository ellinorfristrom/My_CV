Education

  • BA(Hons) | Multimedia Journalism Oct 2017 - Jul 2021 | UK

    Accredited by the National Council for the Training of Journalists (NCTJ) and the Broadcast Journalism Training Council (BJTC).

  • Year Abroad | Journalism Jul 2019 - Jun 2020 | Australia

    Part of my degree at the University of Essex. Besides journalism, I also studied anthropology, creative arts and Australian studies.

Work Experience

  • Oxford Mail | Oct 2021 - Dec 2021

    Worked as a reporter at the Oxford Mail, where I conducted interviews and wrote articles. I was also in charge of their weekly sister paper, the Bicester Advertiser, and made sure that the paper was filled each week. I was also running the Facebook page for the Bicester Advertiser.

  • Mojo News | Aug 2019 - Jun 2020

    I wrote a couple of articles for the student newspaper Mojo News at Monash University. I also produced TV packages for one of their programs Mojo Weekly News, which aired every week on channel 31 in Melbourne.

  • Aceville Publication | Oct 2018 - Nov 2018

    Worked on Fridays over five weeks and wrote articles for their magazine on "Chains and Franchises". I also helped them develop their social media profile & webpage to promote another magazine.

  • BBC One - Look East | May 2018

    Five-day work placement in Norwich. As a second-year journalism student, this placement was very crucial for me, which gave me a better understanding of what it's like to work in a professional newsroom.
